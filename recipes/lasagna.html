The Best Recipe for Lasagna

Beautiful homemade lasagna

This lasagna takes some time to make, but it's definitely worth the wait. Nothing will feel as satisfying as when you're finally finished laboring over it and you get to taste the quality of your own homemade dish.

Ingredients

  • Meat: You will need italian sausage and ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce and Paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings: Parsley, basil leaves, salt, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest.
