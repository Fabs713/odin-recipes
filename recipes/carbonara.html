The Ultimate Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • sea salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Chop pancetta, grate pecorino and parmesan and mix them together
  3. Add salt and spaghetti to boiling water for 10 minutes
  4. Squash and peel the garlic before adding it to a pan with pancetta
  5. Add pasta to the pan and mix in the cheese and eggs
  6. Cook until finished and then serve.
